Airport Drop-off Charges

Important information about drop-off charges at London airports

Heathrow Airport (LHR)

Heathrow Drop Off Charge has implemented a £7.00 drop-off charge Up to 15 minutes for vehicles using the terminal forecourts. This charge applies to all private vehicles including taxis and minicabs.

Important: To avoid this charge, we recommend using the free drop-off points located in the Long Stay car parks. We can arrange pick-up/drop-off at these locations at no extra cost to you.

Stansted Airport (STN)

Stansted Airport has implemented a £7.00 drop-off charge for vehicles using the terminal forecourt. This charge applies to all private vehicles including taxis and minicabs learn more about Stansted Drop Off Charge .

Important: To avoid this charge, we recommend using the free drop-off points located in the Mid Stay car park. We can arrange pick-up/drop-off at these locations at no extra cost to you.

Other London Airports

Gatwick, Luton, and London City airports currently do not have drop-off charges for private vehicles.
